About Matrix

Matrix 1.0 , accomplished to be an official hackathon of Binary Cumates. This hackathon will allow the participants from all over India to bring in their ideas and compete to win amazing prizes.Here through this Hackathon, an amazing platform is provided to the flamboyant students to evince their ideas and emerge at the top.

ROADMAP

  • Result of Preliminary Round will out on 9th Aug.
  • Final round will begin on 12th Aug and it will end on 14th Aug.
  • Final result will out on 14th Aug.

FAQ

Matrix is planned in online mode. The whole event will take place virtually.

Matrix is open to all undergraduate and high school students around the world. There is no prerequisite for the participants. We hope to keep the hackathon beginner-friendly so that everyone can learn something from this hackathon.

Minimum of 2 members are required in a team. If you are not able to form your team then you can find a team member in our discord server.

This hackathon is beginner friendly and the most important thing "if you will not start then you can not learn"

If you have any more questions, feel free to reach out to us at binarycumates@gmail.com and we will try to respond as quickly as we can.

About BCM

Binary Cumates is a non profitable student community working for the betterment of students in Chandigarh University. We are providing a different set of values in addition of skill development of college students. This platform is open for every student who is member of Chandigarh university. This platform will not only provide you all to enhance your skill and to boost your confidence.
